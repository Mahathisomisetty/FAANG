United Kingdom is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the world, showcasing its rich history and architectural brilliance. Tower Bridge in London stands majestically over the River Thames, especially stunning when illuminated at night. Nearby, the famous Elizabeth Tower—commonly known as Big Ben—continues to be a symbol of British heritage with its striking clock face. On the northeast coast, St. Mary’s Lighthouse offers a serene maritime view, perched on a tidal island surrounded by rocky shores. In Scotland, Glasgow Cathedral stands as a magnificent example of Gothic architecture and medieval craftsmanship. Together, these landmarks reflect the cultural depth and timeless beauty of the UK.
